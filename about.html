Sunlife Meditech was founded with one clear purpose, to make medical procurement simpler, more reliable and more consistent for hospitals everywhere.

Our Story

Who We Are

Sunlife Meditech is a dedicated medical product supplier committed to equipping hospitals and healthcare facilities with everything they need, under one roof and one trusted brand.

From surgical essentials and operation theatre equipment to personal hygiene products and general medical supplies, our curated portfolio is designed to meet the diverse and critical demands of modern healthcare institutions.

We operate with a singular focus: make procurement simple, reliable and consistent for hospitals. No compromises on quality. No delays in delivery. No need to chase multiple vendors for different product categories.

We are a key supplier to government institutions and major hospital chains. The Sunlife brand stands for consistency. Every product that carries our name has been selected and quality-checked to meet the standards that hospitals and their patients deserve.
